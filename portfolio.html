Gas Geek

Gas Geek is a sophisticated web application that calculates the average gas consumption based on the user's vehicle choice. It was developed using a skillful blend of React, Tailwind, TypeScript, and Next.js. This project involved the successful integration of 3 APIs, including Cars by API-Ninjas, Google Image API, and US gas price API, which underpins the accurate retrieval and processing of relevant vehicle data. Through this endeavor, I have demonstrated a potent ability to intersect web development and real-world problem-solving, reflecting the transformative potential of technology in aiding everyday decision-making.
